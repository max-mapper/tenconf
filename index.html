21st & 22nd September '12, New York City

10 hours. 10 minute talks. Everybody speaks.

TenConf is a two day conference of 10 minute talks. Every attendee will give a 10 minute talk. There are no restrictions on the range of content: anything about culture, art and technology (or the intersections between) is fair game.
